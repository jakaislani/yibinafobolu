Best Slider Buns We Found in a Taste Test

May 23, 2024
blog

The best slider buns are made by King’s Hawaiian. After tasting a ton of different rolls for sliders, we can confidently say that King’s Hawaiian is making the only slider rolls worth buying.

It’s no surprise, though. They also make some of our favorite hot dog and hamburger buns. When you want a bread product that will improve your sandwich-adjacent meals, King’s Hawaiian is, well, king. And before you say anything: No! This is not sponsored. We just really love King’s Hawaiian.

Best Sweet

King’s Hawaiian Sweet Slider BunsWalmart

When I tell you that King’s Hawaiian makes the best slider buns, I’m not joking. These are absolutely perfect. They’re sweet without tasting like dessert. They sop up sauce like a dream. They are pillowy soft. These are the rolls for sliders. I want to put ham and Swiss and honey mustard on these and then pop them into the oven for a toasty little snack. They would also make the cutest little chicken salad sandwiches for a book club snack or Bachelorette watch party spread. Buy these now. Thank me later.

Best of the Best

King’s Hawaiian Pretzel SlidersKing’s Hawaiian

The King’s Hawaiian pretzel buns are one of our favorite hamburger buns, so it makes sense that we’d also love the pretzel rolls for sliders. These are simply perfect. I cannot imagine a better bun for a little burger than these. They are soft, lightly sweet, and have the perfect amount of chew. I promise that your family and friends will be blown away if you bust out these slider buns at a party. They are truly the best slider rolls out there. We love you, King’s Hawaiian!

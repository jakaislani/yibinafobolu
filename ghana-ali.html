Ghana Ali Raza, popularly known as Ghana Ali- is a Pakistani TV and film actress. Her skills are not just limited to acting, yet she is a great model and has done several modeling assignments for notable brands. Also, she has worked in numerous TV serials and also performed in some movies as well. While her fans sing the praise for her wonderful acting skills. She rose to fame after appearing in the serial “Sangdil”. While she made her acting debut with a musical-drama “Rangreza” which was released in the year 2017. She was born on 26th January 1994, Lahore.

Career

In 2015, Ghana Ali took the start of her career with a serial “Ishqaaway” and in the same year, she gave amazing performances in the serials “Ap Ke Liye”, “Sangdil”, and “Dharkan”. In 2016, she also worked in “Saya-e-Dewar Bhi Nahi”, “Besharam”, “Ahsas”, and “Jaan’nisar”. In 2017, she acted in “Be-Inteha”, “Sun Yaara”,” Sawera”, “Bedard Saiyaan”, and “Agar Tum Saath Ho” are notable. While in the same year, she performed in the musical drama “Rangreza” for which she got huge acclaim. In 2018, she worked in “Ustaani Jee”, “Kis Din Mera Viyah Hoga”, “Saaya”, and others. In the same year, she appeared in the film “Maan Jao Na”. While in the year 2019, she has worked in “Hania”, and “Choti Choti Baten and others. In the same year, she worked in the movies “Kaaf Kangana” and “Sirf Tum Hi To Ho”. In 2020, she is presently working in the most-watched serial “Dil-Ruba” starring Hania Amir and others. However, she has done a remarkable job as she is blessed with outstanding acting prowess.

Achievements

The young actress, Ghana Ali has achieved much fame and huge recognition during her career life and still struggling to gain more success in the future.

Previously in Oshi no Ko Chapter 120

In the 120th chapter of “Oshi no Ko,” titled “Lack of Ability,” Ruby’s troubles with her busy schedule and her desire to become a better actress are explored. At the beginning of the chapter, Ruby is struggling with the difficulties posed by her too-busy schedule. She is so dedicated to doing a great job in her role that she stays up late every night memorizing her lines. But Producer Kaburagi tells Miyako that Ruby needs serious acting lessons, so her best efforts go to waste.

Ruby, who is less experienced than her co-stars, goes to Kana Arima for advice because she wants to be able to elicit real feelings in her acting. Because of her history as Sarina, Ruby finds this suggestion extremely difficult to implement. Moreover, she struggles with ambivalent feelings toward Marina Tendouji, her mother.

Past relationship
